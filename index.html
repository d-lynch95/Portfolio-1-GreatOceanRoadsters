Your guide to one of Australias most scenic drives

Attractions

Kennet Koala Walk

One of the best places in Australia to spot koalas in their natural surroundings. Grab a coffee at Koala Cafe and take a walk up the old river road to spot the koalas in the trees

Twelve Apostles

An absolute must on your visit to the Great Ocean Road. Bask in the views of these amazing sea stacks. Make sure you don't forget your camera!

Hopkins Falls

Measuring 90 metres wide and plunging 12 metres over dark basalt rocks, the curtain-like hopkins falls are a must see on your road trip.

Catch a wave

With over 200km of coastline you're spoiled for choice in this surfers paradise. Either find a secluded cove or hang 10 at one of the busier beaches.
